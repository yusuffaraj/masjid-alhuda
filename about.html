About Masjid Al-Huda

Masjid Al-Huda is a mosque located in the heart of Leicester. Our mission is to provide a welcoming and inclusive space for all members of the community to come together in prayer and to learn about the teachings of Islam.

Our mosque offers a range of services and programs for people of all ages, including daily prayers, Quran classes, and community events.

We strive to create an environment that promotes unity, compassion, and understanding among people of different backgrounds and beliefs.
