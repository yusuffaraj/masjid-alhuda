Welcome to Masjid AL-Huda

We are delighted to have you visit our mosque. Our goal is to provide a peaceful and welcoming environment where Muslims can come together for prayer, education, and community events. We offer a variety of programs for children, youth, and adults, and we encourage everyone to get involved and make a positive impact in our community. Please explore our website to learn more about our services, events, and how you can support our mission.
